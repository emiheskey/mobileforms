Why CrowdForce?

Economies in Africa are experiencing double digit growths which is attracting the attention of companies across the world. A continent of 1 billion people with a consumer market of over 1 trillion dollars. Businesses face three key challenges when trying to access this market.

Cost

The cost of remittances is punitively high. Transferring money via banks and traditional international money transfers operators could cost from 7-12% of the value transferred.

Visibility

Over 40% of remittances are intended for specific uses like food, clothing, medicine, school fees or utility bill payments. Senders want to ensure that their money is being utilized for its intended purpose.

CrowdForce Ecosystem

The CrowdForce platform dwells at the heart of the CrowdForce ecosystem, providing an avenue for companies and other users of market data to conduct penetrative market research in remote areas. The other participant in the CrowdForce ecosystem is the field agent who is responsible for doing the legwork involved in obtaining the value market data. These agents are paid in CRD tokens which is the native token of the platform. The transaction flow of the CrowForce platform is as follows

  • Sign up as a Field agent in your area
  • Perform simple and complex survey task
  • Get paid in CRD tokens
  • Exchange CRD for Flat Currency or use to pay for goods or services at your nearest Mobile Money Agent (A fellow licensed CrowdForce Agent)
