HR Circle sponsored by Sorterbox teams up with Mobile Forms

In HR, accurate data is vital to making strategic decisions. HR Circle recommends the use of Mobile Forms for many data collect points throughout the cycle of employee in your organization.

Hire request survey.

So your organization is growing! Why not have an organized approach in gauging the potential headcount increase from departmental heads each quarter with questions like "what will be the return of investment of this new hire?" and "what would happen if we do not hire this individual?"

Recruitment process satisfaction survey.

Create a short and simple survey asking candidates their views on your current recruitment process. Bonus points if you send this survey to a candidate who was rejected.

Employee Engagement survey.

Do you sense the employee engagement has declined, if so build out a short survey to find out why.

Exit interview survey.

Have terminated employees fill out an exit interview survey and compile data on things you should fix and work on to make sure every employee you bring on had a meaningful time at your company.
