Mobile Data Platform

Collect data you can trust, online, offline, in the office and on the Field.

Features

Collect data Offline

Mobile Forms works offline so your work never has to stop. Your data will be validated offline and syncronized the next time the device gets connected.

Easy to Use

Easy to use our drag and drop formbuilder. Include validations, Skip logics and over 11 question types including the ability to capture images, gps and text.

Export Ready

All your data is collected into one central database available 24X7 and export automatically in excel, csv, PDF or integrate with your database in the office

Fill your forms anywhere

The mobile platform is a fully intergated solution for all you data collection needs. Your employees, team members, field agents and customers can fill the same form on your website or portal using their laptop or desktop.
